Why We Win

4 Compounding Moats

🦠 K-Factor = 1.65 Virality

You can't sign a document alone. Every doc invites 2–8 people. Each signer becomes a registered user. Each user invites more. CAC structurally compresses to ~$8.

DocuSign: $25–45/mo enterprise pricing. SignFlow: $4.99/mo for individuals + Pay-per-Use. Structurally impossible for incumbents to match without cannibalizing their enterprise revenue.
